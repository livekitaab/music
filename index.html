Who We Are

A Library Built
for Music Minds

LiveKitaab Music is a curated digital library dedicated to musical scholarship — from classical theory to modern ethnomusicology. Every title is chosen to deepen your understanding of the art of sound.

Whether you are a conservatory student, a working musician, or simply someone who loves music deeply, our collection has something to illuminate your journey.
